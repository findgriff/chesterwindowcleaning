About

One person. One round. Done properly.

I run Chester Window Cleaner as a one-person operation — the person who quotes your price is the person who cleans your windows.

I clean residential windows across Chester and Deeside on a regular 4–6 week round. Pure water, ground level, no ladders — every clean covers your windows, frames, sills and doors.

Being a one-person operation means a few things work differently here. The prices are published, and they're the prices you pay. You get a text the night before each visit, from me. And if something isn't right, you tell me once and I fix it — there's no office to phone.

If that's what you're after and you're in CH1–CH5, get a price from the widget and I'll be in touch within four working hours.
