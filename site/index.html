The method

Pure water. Nothing else.

I use a soft brush and pure water — the same water you'd drink, just with the dissolved minerals taken out. It grabs the dirt, dries leaving nothing behind, and means no chemicals run down your walls or onto your plants.

Everything is reached from the ground with a carbon-fibre pole — including upstairs windows and the awkward bits over conservatory roofs.

Questions

Asked all the time

What if it's raining?

Pure water cleans through light rain — modern water-fed pole work isn't disrupted by it. Heavy rain or storm, I'll postpone and let you know.

Do I need to be in?

No. As long as I can reach the back of the property, you don't need to be home. I'll text the night before so it's never a surprise.

How do I pay?

Bank transfer is easiest — details are on the receipt I send after each clean.

Can I cancel?

Any time. There's no contract — just text me and I'll take you off the round.
