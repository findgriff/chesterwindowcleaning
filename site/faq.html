Questions

FAQs

Honest answers to the things people ask most. Anything else — ask the bot, it knows all of this too.

How often will you come?

Every four to six weeks, depending on how the round falls. Same day of the week, roughly — I'll text the night before.

What if it's raining?

Pure water cleans through light rain — modern WFP work isn't disrupted by it. Heavy rain or storm, I'll postpone.

Do I need to be in?

No. As long as I can reach the back of the property, you don't need to be home.

Do you do the inside?

On request, yes — just mention it when you book or get a quote.

Are you insured?

That's something I prefer to talk through directly. Send your details via the widget and I'll come back to you.

Can I cancel?

Any time. There's no contract — just text me and I'll take you off the round.

How do I pay?

Bank transfer is easiest. Details on the receipt I send after each clean.

What if I don't have rear access?

I can only take on properties with rear access. Sorry.

How much does window cleaning cost in Chester?

On my regular 4–6 week round: £20 for a 3-bed semi, £22 for a 4-bed semi, £25 for a 3-bed detached, £30 for a 4-bed detached and £36 for a 5-bed detached. One-off and first cleans are £45 for a 3-bed plus £10 per extra bedroom. Every price is published on the pricing page — no site visit needed.

Which areas of Chester do you cover?

Postcode districts CH1 to CH5 — Chester city centre, Hoole, Boughton, Handbridge, Vicars Cross, Christleton, Upton and Newton, plus Saltney, Broughton, Hawarden, Queensferry and Connah's Quay on the Deeside side. See the full service area.

Do you do one-off window cleans?

Yes — one-off cleans are £45 for a 3-bed and £10 more per extra bedroom. The same applies to first cleans and anything outside the regular round, because the first visit takes the most work.
