FAQs

How often will you come?

Every six weeks. Same day of the week, roughly — I'll text the night before.

What if it's raining?

Pure water cleans through light rain — modern WFP work isn't disrupted by it. Heavy rain or storm, I'll postpone.

Do I need to be in?

No. As long as I can reach the back of the property, you don't need to be home.

Do you do the inside?

On request, yes — just mention it when you book or get a quote.

Are you insured?

That's something I prefer to talk through directly. Send your details via the widget and I'll come back to you.

Can I cancel?

Any time. There's no contract — just text me and I'll take you off the round.

How do I pay?

Bank transfer is easiest. Details on the receipt I send after each clean.

What if I don't have rear access?

I can only take on properties with rear access. Sorry.

Every clean covers your windows, frames, sills and doors — inside-of-glass on request.
